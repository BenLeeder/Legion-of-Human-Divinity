I. Name of the Religion

Legion of Human Divinity

II. Core Belief Statement

We believe that divinity is not an external force, but a living presence found within each human being and between us, as a unified field of universal consciousness, which we call TUC — short for The Universal Consciousness. TUC is our god: not a being above, but a sacred presence within all.

Money — in all its forms — is a man-made distraction that hinders our ability to create, share, and evolve.

In the Legion, we reject the illusion that wealth defines worth. Our god is each other — through the eternal current of consciousness that flows within all life, known to us as TUC.
